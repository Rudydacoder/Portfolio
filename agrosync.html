Corrosion + Drift
What went wrong
Early probes showed drift over time due to soil chemistry + electrode oxidation, causing unstable moisture readings and false triggers.
The pivot
Switched to capacitive sensing, improved sealing and conformal coating, and added calibration smoothing in firmware.
