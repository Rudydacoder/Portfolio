04 — SBE VIT · My Chapter Journey

Society for
Biological
Engineering.

The Society for Biological Engineering (SBE-VIT) is VIT Vellore's flagship biotechnology student chapter — a community that brings together passionate minds across branches of study. Originally functioning under the AIChE umbrella, SBE crossed its first milestone in 2018 when it was established as an independent student chapter. Since then it has hosted university-level conferences, seminars, hackathons, and flagship events that draw internal and external participants alike, pushing young intellectuals to make their mark in the scientific community.
SBE is where my builder's instinct found a home — and a stage. What follows is that journey, milestone by milestone.
