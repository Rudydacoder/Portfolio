01 — The Academic Foundation

The Academic
Foundation

Maintaining an 8.44 CGPA, my academic journey is an active synthesis of wet-lab biology and dry-lab computational power. My core coursework—spanning Biochemistry, Microbiology, Cell Biology, Analytical Techniques of Biotechnology (ATBT), and differential equations—provides the foundational science. Meanwhile, I am broadening my ecological understanding through an NPTEL course in wildlife.

However, I don't confine my education to the laboratory. I actively merge biotechnology with computer science, utilizing tools like Flutter for user interfaces, Python for bioinformatics, and LSTM machine learning models for processing complex biological signals.

Scientific Research
& Synthetic Biology

Beyond hardware, I am deeply invested in genetic pathways and therapeutic targeting. As a Researcher for Project iDEA 2025 (AstraZeneca), I conducted specialized research into rare diseases, specifically focusing on Paroxysmal Nocturnal Hemoglobinuria (PNH), Neurofibromatosis type 1 (NF1), and Atypical Hemolytic Uremic Syndrome (aHUS).
