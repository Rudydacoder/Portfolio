02 — Architecture

How it
works.

01
Sense

Velostat polymer changes resistance under pressure. Nine zones across the plantar surface feed voltage readings into an ESP32 at 60 frames per second.

Hardware
02
Interpret

A low-pass filter strips jitter. The system computes Center of Pressure, postural sway velocity, and a Berg Balance Scale proxy — all in real time, no cloud dependency.

Signal Processing
03
Engage

Sensor data drives Neuro-Glider — an arcade game where your ankle is the controller. Sustained balance triggers Overdrive Mode. Patients stay. Drop-off rates fall.
